Cafe Panna: Where authentic Italian Ice Cream, sundaes and soft serve are all in the same world!

OUR MISSION: "ICE CREAM TO TABLE"

"Less is More!"

"Extra. Extra sweet, extra salty, extra rich, extra fresh, extra crunchy but somehow just a millimeter shy of too much." — New York Magazine

Founded by Halli Meyer (raised in Manhattan) became inspired by Italian gelato. She wnated to establish cafe panna as the best ice cream, she also hosts Trattoria Panna (pasta pop-ups).

In-house ice cream and sundaes w/ ingredients that constantly change. Ingredients are from Union Square greenmarket and imported pistachios and olive oil from Sicily.

We uses Panna (light sweet whipped cream from Sicily) on every ice cream and sundae.

Hours: Wednesday - Sunday 1:30-9:30pm
